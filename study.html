2nd declension has both masculine (servus type) and neuter (templum type) patterns. Neuter Nom/Acc Sg is -um, Pl -a; otherwise follows the 2nd declension pattern.

Case Uses

  • Nominative: Subject, Complement
  • Genitive: Possession
  • Dative: Indirect Object (IO), OSV
  • Accusative: Direct Object (DO), Object of Preposition (OP)
  • Ablative: Object of Preposition (OP)
